新的一年学会说 no

First is, if I’m faced with a difficult choice such as should I marry this person?
首先，如果我面临一个困难的选择，比如我应该嫁给这个人吗？

Should I take this job? Should I buy this house? Should I move to this city?
我应该接受这份工作吗？我应该购买这所房子吗？我应该搬到这个城市吗？

Should I go in business with this person? If you cannot decide, the answer is no.
我应该和这个人合伙创业吗？如果你无法做出决定，答案就是不。

And the reason is because modern society is full of options. There are tons and tons of options.
原因是因为现代社会充满了选择。有大量的选择。

We live in a planet of 7 billion people. We are connected to everybody on the internet.
我们生活在一个有 70 亿人口的星球上。我们与互联网上的每个人都连接在一起。

There are hundreds of thousands of careers available to you. There are so many choices.
对你来说有数以千计的职业可选择。选择实在太多了。

You’re biologically not built to realize how many choices there are.
从生物学角度来看，你并不适应意识到有这么多的选择。

Because we evolved in tribes of 150 people. Where if you pass up one choice, the second thing never comes along.
因为我们在由 150 人组成的部落中进化。在那里，如果你放过一个选择，第二个事情永远不会出现。

Also, when you choose something, you get locked in for a long time.
而且，当你做出选择时，你就会被长时间锁定。

If you start a business, that’s 10 years. If you get into a relationship, that’s 5 years.
如果你创办一家公司，那就是 10 年。如果你进入一段关系，那就是 5 年。

Maybe more. You move to a city that’s 10-20 years.
可能更长。你搬到一个城市可能要 10-20 年。

So these are very, very long-lived decisions.
所以这些是非常、非常持久的决定。

So it’s very, very important that you only say yes when you are pretty certain.
因此，只有在你相当确定的时候才说 “是” 是非常、非常重要的。
